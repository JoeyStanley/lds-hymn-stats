Quick Facts

I currently have data from 20,324 sacrament meetings. If you were to attend that many, it would take roughly 423.4 years—so, more than a couple lifetimes’ worth of data. It’s also the equivalent of the entire 38-year lifetime of the 1985 hymnal in 11.14 wards. This data comes from 984 wards, mostly in the United States. Across all these meetings, there are 70,229 hymns. See here for a detailed breakdown of how this data is distributed across time and space, as well as an explanation of where it call came from.
