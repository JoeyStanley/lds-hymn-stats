This page just has a summary of all the hymns and their frequency stats, including the new hymns. I will update this page regularly as I collect more data.

Quick Facts

I currently have data from 36,762 sacrament meetings. If you were to attend that many, it would take roughly 765.9 years—so, more than a couple lifetimes’ worth of data. It’s also the equivalent of the entire 39-year lifetime of the 1985 hymnal in 19.64 wards. This data comes from 2,501 wards, mostly in the United States. Across all these meetings, there are 123,865 hymns. See here for a detailed breakdown of how this data is distributed across time and space, as well as an explanation of where it call came from.

Frequency Table

Note that songs from the Children’s Songbook are listed with their page number plus 2000. So, We’ll Bring the World His Truth is on page 172 but I’ve listed it as hymn 2172. Also, when a non-English speaking ward sings a hymn that’s not found in the English hymnal, I find the translation on Samuel Bradshaw’s amazing SingPraises.net and sequentially number them in the order I see them starting with 500. So, if a ward sings O, Canada, it’s listed here as hymn 501.
