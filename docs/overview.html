Quick Facts

I currently have data from 21,481 sacrament meetings. If you were to attend that many, it would take roughly 447.5 years—so, more than a couple lifetimes’ worth of data. It’s also the equivalent of the entire 39-year lifetime of the 1985 hymnal in 11.47 wards. This data comes from 1059 wards, mostly in the United States. Across all these meetings, there are 73,827 hymns. See here for a detailed breakdown of how this data is distributed across time and space, as well as an explanation of where it call came from.
