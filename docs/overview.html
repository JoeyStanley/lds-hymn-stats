Quick Facts

I currently have data from 19,118 sacrament meetings. If you were to attend that many, it would take roughly 398.3 years—so, more than a couple lifetimes’ worth of data. It’s also the equivalent of the entire 38-year lifetime of the 1985 hymnal in 10.48 wards. This data comes from 957 wards, mostly in the United States. Across all these meetings, there are 66,267 hymns.
