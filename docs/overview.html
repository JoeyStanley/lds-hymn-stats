Quick Facts

I currently have data from 19,285 sacrament meetings. If you were to attend that many, it would take roughly 401.8 years—so, more than a couple lifetimes’ worth of data. It’s also the equivalent of the entire 38-year lifetime of the 1985 hymnal in 10.57 wards. This data comes from 965 wards, mostly in the United States. Across all these meetings, there are 66,861 hymns.
