Quick Facts

I currently have data from 72,415 sacrament meetings. If you were to attend that many, it would take about 1509 years—so, more than a couple lifetimes’ worth of data. It’s also the equivalent of the entire 40-year lifetime of the 1985 hymnal in 38 wards. This data comes from 3,037 wards, mostly in the United States. Across all these meetings, there are 231,187 hymns. See here for a detailed breakdown of how this data is distributed across time and space, as well as an explanation of where it call came from.
