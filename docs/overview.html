Quick Facts

I currently have data from 18,810 sacrament meetings. If you were to attend that many, it would take roughly 391.9 years—so, more than a couple lifetimes’ worth of data. It’s also the equivalent of the entire 38-year lifetime of the 1985 hymnal in 10 wards. This data comes from 944 wards, mostly in the United States. Across all these meetings, there is data about 65,297 hymns.
