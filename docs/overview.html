Quick Facts

I currently have data from 18,968 sacrament meetings. If you were to attend that many, it would take roughly 395.2 years—so, more than a couple lifetimes’ worth of data. It’s also the equivalent of the entire 38-year lifetime of the 1985 hymnal in 10 wards. This data comes from 946 wards, mostly in the United States. Across all these meetings, there is data about 65,757 hymns.
