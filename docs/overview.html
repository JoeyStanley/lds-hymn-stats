Quick Facts

I currently have data from 23,713 sacrament meetings. If you were to attend that many, it would take roughly 494 years—so, more than a couple lifetimes’ worth of data. It’s also the equivalent of the entire 39-year lifetime of the 1985 hymnal in 12.67 wards. This data comes from 1,208 wards, mostly in the United States. Across all these meetings, there are 81,132 hymns. See here for a detailed breakdown of how this data is distributed across time and space, as well as an explanation of where it call came from.
